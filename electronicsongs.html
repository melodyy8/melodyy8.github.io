Dance of Euphoria

Number of Pieces:

1

Length:

4:35

Description:

I wrote this song using LMMS, and it took me over 500 days to finish! I started writing it at the beginning of 2020, but had to stop because I was busy with school and had other personal issues. I started working on the song again in May 2021 and finally finished it! I called it "Dance of Euphoria" because this song reminds me of my personal journey and how much happier I am now! It reminds me that things get better, and the positive/upbeat sound gives me optimism and fills me with hope.
