Welcome to the marching ensemble page! Here you can find my field shows for marching bands and drum corps! You can click on the links to view the PDFs and audio, and I hope you enjoy!

Supernova (Marching Band Show)

Number of Pieces:

3 (Nebula, Star, Supernova)

Length:

Total: 7:38
Nebula: 2:00
Star: 2:26
Supernova: 3:12

Show Length:

~8:00

Description:

This was my first ever field show! I wrote it for marching band, and it's space themed. I used music from a variety of sources, like Kevin Macleod, Gravity (2013), and Super Mario Galaxy 2. The three movements represent a star's journey through life, and I tried to create a lot of dynamic and tempo contrast to make the show more lively.

Perfectly Balanced (Drum Corps Show)

Number of Pieces:

3 (Parts I, II, III)

Length:

Total: 8:19
Part I: 3:29
Part II: 2:54
Part III: 1:56

Show Length:

~8:40

Description:

This was my first drum corps show! I wrote it during my freshman year of college, and I based it off of the Avengers. The three parts use music from Marvel movies, and I also included some original parts to link the different sections together. I tried to use more complicated rhythms and notes because drum corps is at a higher level than marching band, so I wanted my music to reflect that.
