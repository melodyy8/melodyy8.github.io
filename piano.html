Playtime Suite

Number of Pieces:

4 (Parts 1-4)

Length:

Total: 5:07
Part 1: 1:47
Part 2: 0:56
Part 3: 1:00
Part 4: 1:24

Difficulty:

Beginner

Description:

The Playtime Suite is made up of 4 beginner-level pieces, each one relatively short and simple. I wrote these pieces when I was 13, using manuscript paper, and I recently transcribed them online using MuseScore. These pieces evoke feelings of childhood, nostalgia, and fun.

"Sonatina" in C Major No. 1 Vivace

Number of Pieces:

1

Length:

2:24

Difficulty:

Advanced

Description:

This song was my first attempt at writing piano music in a more classical format. It's not really a sonatina, but at the time, I didn't know a better name for it. I struggled to finish the piece for a long time, but I recently managed to write an ending for it that I thought was fitting!

Brainiac Maniac (for Two Pianos)

Number of Pieces:

1

Length:

3:33

Difficulty:

Advanced

Description:

This was the first piano battle I wrote, using NoteFlight. The original song is from Plants vs. Zombies, which was a very popular game when I was younger. I added drums and cymbals to keep the rhythm and give a little more body to the sound.
