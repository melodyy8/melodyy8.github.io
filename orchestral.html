Yi Dodgeball (Gameplay Music)

Number of Pieces:

1

Length:

3:44

Description:

I wrote this song on Musescore to accompany a game I made using Snap! to prepare for my computer science class. As you can tell by the title, it was a dodgeball game. I made really cute creatures instead of balls, and a scary final boss. I wanted the music to play during the game, so I used a simpler chord progression and switched the instruments every once in a while so it would match the flow of the game. To make the sound more dramatic for the final boss, I added a choir and tubular bells, and an electric guitar solo using the blues scale.
